About us

The movie, "Lost in the arts center" was made as a part of the 30 minutes film festival. It features a common problem all of the students who are attending classes in the arts center have faced, which is getting lost in the vast plain of all the rooms of the arts center.

The creators of the movie wanted to present the issue with a somewhat satirical movie showing how a girl (one of the creators) is in the end late to the class she came early to. The hallways are presented like a never-ending maze that always leads to the same destination.
